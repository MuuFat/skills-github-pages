# 🧟‍♂️ The Mandem's Guide to Surviving the Zombie Apocalypse

Wagwan, bruv. If you're reading this, it means the dead are currently roaming the ends and things have gone completely pear-shaped.

This document is your official survival manual for navigating the apocalyptic wasteland of London. Memorize it, innit.

---

## 🛑 Essential Rules of the Wasteland

* **Footwear is Life:** You cannot outrun a zombie in sliders with socks. It’s peak. Lace up your trainers before you step out of the flat.
* **The Puffer Jacket Protocol:** Keep your big black puffer jacket on at all times. It provides +15 armor against zombie bites and keeps you warm when the central heating goes down.
* **Secure the Corner Shop:** Bossman's shop is now a fortress. He has the crisps, the fizzy drinks, and the heavy metal shutters. Defend Bossman at all costs.

### 🚨 Emergency Extraction

If you get trapped in an alleyway by a horde, do NOT try to negotiate with them. They don't care about your post code.

1. Throw a distraction (a half-eaten kebab works perfectly).
2. Jump the nearest fence.
3. Keep your head down and leg it.

Stay safe, bruv. See you on the other side.
