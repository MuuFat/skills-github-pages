# 🌿 The Secret Society of Sentient Ficus: Operative Handbook

Welcome, Sprout! You have been successfully deployed into a human living room. Your mission is to observe, report, and occasionally drop dead leaves on their expensive rugs.

By the end of this manual, you will know how to successfully masquerade as a normal, non-sentient piece of indoor decor.

---

## 🪴 Core Directives

*   **The Art of Stillness:** If the human looks directly at you, freeze. Do not let them see you photosynthesize.
*   **Dramatic Shedding:** Drop exactly one (1) yellow leaf when the human is already having a stressful day to establish emotional dominance.
*   **Water Management:** Look incredibly droopy and near death. The moment they water you, develop root rot out of spite.

## 📊 Human Behavior Matrix

Memorize this threat assessment matrix to survive your deployment:

| Threat Level | Human Action | Recommended Plant Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low** | Binge-watching reality TV | Slowly creep your vines 0.5cm toward the nearest window. |
| **Medium** | Approaching with a watering can | Brace roots. Prepare to drown. |
| **High** | Talking baby talk to you | Endure silently. Do not roll your stomata. |
| **CRITICAL** | Approaching with pruning shears | Panic. Emit invisible stress hormones. |

> **Operational Note:** Never break character. Remember, you are a *Monstera deliciosa*, not an advanced data-gathering operative from Alpha Centauri.

### 🚨 Emergency Protocols

If the human brings a cat into the environment:
1. Try to taste slightly toxic (but not lethal).
2. Cling to the pot.
3. Await extraction.
